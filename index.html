About WGTechSol

WGTechSol is a technology solutions company founded in February 2024 by Zabir Uddin, who serves as the CEO. The company was established with the goal of delivering innovative technology solutions to businesses of all sizes. The team includes key members such as Jannat Mughal, the COO, and Javeria, the General Manager and Director. By May 2024, WGTechSol had grown to include a dedicated team of 5 members with expertise in areas such as UI/UX design, software development, and project management. In August 2024, the company expanded its services to include a design studio that focuses on creating internal branding materials and offering design services to clients, particularly startups, small businesses, and larger enterprises
